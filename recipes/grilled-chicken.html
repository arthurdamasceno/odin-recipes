Grilled Chicken

Description

Grilled Chicken is a delicious recipe to make for lunch!

Furthermore, it is a healthy and fast recipe.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Salt
  • Lemon
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Put some olive oil in the pan
  2. Put the chicken in the pan
  3. Add lemon and salt to season
  4. Turn the chicken when it is to your liking and wait until it is cooked
